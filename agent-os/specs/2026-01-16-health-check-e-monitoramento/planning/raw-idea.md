**Feature Name:** Health Check e Monitoramento

**Description:**
Endpoints de status dos serviços, alertas de falha do listener e dashboard de saúde do sistema.

## Context

This is part of SIDOT - a system for organ donation notification. This feature will:

- Support ops/devops monitoring
